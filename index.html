About

Hello, my name is Rick Lozano. I am currently a truck driver looking to escape and move into a better work life. I have been into IT type work for about 25 years, from building computers to general repairs, upgrading, virus removals and info backups. I have in the past 3 or so years been working on learning programming (java, c#, and python). My goal aside from getting out of driving is to eventually make a video game but ultimately, get into a programming based carrier. After watching some videos and taking a few courses, I have realized I enjoy front end web development.

For a good 27 years of my life I have been playing music. I taught myself piano being inspired by Rachmaninov, and taught myself bass after listening to Primus. Of course I see the top of the mountain in anything that interest me and I do everything I can to get there in the fastest most efficient way possible. My personal best achievement with music is writing and recording an album with a friend of mine. A good portion of my day is spent listening to music.

I have played games since I was about 6 starting with the Atari 2600. I have ran many table top games such as Dungeons and Dragons (2.5e and 3.5 mainly), Shadowrun, and some of the White Wolf games. I have created my own table top game systems and worlds over the past 25 or so years. The desire to get into programming is to create these worlds I have trapped in my head, so more people can play and experience them.
